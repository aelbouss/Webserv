We build tools for the modern web.

Nexus started as a single server project and grew into a full platform. We're passionate about performance, simplicity, and giving developers full control.

Our story

Born out of frustration with bloated, opaque web servers, Nexus was designed to be transparent, fast, and fully configurable. Every feature exists because a developer needed it.

We believe great infrastructure should be simple to understand and easy to extend. From CGI scripts to custom error pages, you're always in control.
